Overview

mCall — choose who you speak to in a meeting, with end‑to‑end encryption.

Each stream is peer‑to‑peer and encrypted end‑to‑end.

If you do not trust or do not wish to connect to a participant, you can refuse the connection.

You can find the source code [a href="https://github.com/adrianvla/mCall"]here[/a].

Visit [btn link="https://mcall.morisinc.net/build/"]mCall[/btn].

[img src="/assets/img/mcall.webp"][/img]

Features

  • Screensharing
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Peer‑to‑peer connections
  • Ability to decline a connection to a peer
  • Works on mobile and desktop
  • Open source
  • Can host online classes (option to raise your hand)
